Top Destinations in Rajasthan

Udaipur - City of Lakes

Known for its palaces, temples, and lakes. A scenic escape with rich history and culture.

Jaipur - The Pink City

A UNESCO World Heritage site filled with palaces, temples, and forts.

Jaisalmer - The Golden City

Famous for its stunning sandstone architecture and vast desert landscapes.

Pushkar - Holy City

A sacred Hindu pilgrimage site known for its annual camel fair and the serene Pushkar Lake.

Chittorgarh - Historical City

Home to the largest fort in India and rich Rajput history.

Popular Markets in Rajasthan

Johari Bazaar, Jaipur
